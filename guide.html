User Guide

How to use The Pantry

Everything you need to know to find your perfect recipe

```

The Pantry helps you find recipes based on ingredients you already have at home. Tell it what's in your kitchen and it suggests meals — complete with a shopping list for anything missing.

Getting in
Family password
yummy2026$
Enter this when you open the app. You won't need to enter it again on the same device for the rest of your session.
Step by step
1
Add your ingredients
There are three ways to tell the app what you have:
💬Type naturally — use the "Tell us what you have" box at the top. Type something like "I have chicken, garlic, heavy cream and spinach" and the app figures out the ingredients automatically.
👆Browse and tap — scroll through the ingredient categories (Proteins, Produce, Dairy, etc.) and tap anything you have. Tap again to deselect.
✏️Add your own — if something isn't in the list, type it in the "Add your own" box and tap +.
2
Set your preferences (optional)
Tap Recipe Preferences to filter results before searching.
⏱Prep & cook time — choose how much time you have available.
👶Kid friendly — toggle on for mild, family-appropriate meals.
🥗Dietary restrictions — pick from Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Nut-free, Low-carb, or Paleo.
3
Find your recipes
Tap the Find Recipes button — either the one at the bottom of the ingredient panel, or the floating button that appears on screen once you've selected ingredients. A cup animation will appear while results are loading.
🌐Web search mode — toggle "Search the web for real recipes" to get results from sites like Serious Eats, NYT Cooking, and AllRecipes instead of AI-generated recipes.
4
Read your recipe cards
You'll get 4 recipe suggestions, each showing:
Easy / Medium / Hard 👶 Kid friendly 87% match
📊The % match shows how many ingredients you already have. Higher is better. The bar turns red if you need to buy things, green if you have everything.
🔢The nutrition strip shows calories, protein, fat, and carbs per serving at a glance.
5
Open a recipe
Tap any card to expand the full recipe. You'll see the complete ingredient list and step-by-step instructions.
🟢Green dot = you have this ingredient. Red dot = you need to buy it.
⇌Swap button — tap next to any ingredient to get substitution suggestions, or type your own.
👥Servings adjuster — tap − or + to change the serving count. Ingredient quantities and nutrition values scale automatically.
📋Full nutrition facts — tap at the bottom of the recipe to expand a detailed nutrition panel.
6
Use the shopping list
Any ingredient you don't have automatically appears in a shopping list at the bottom of the recipe. Tap an item to check it off as you shop. Tap Print list to print or share it.
Quick reference
🔍
Filter ingredients
Type in the filter box to search the ingredient list quickly.
📂
Collapse categories
Tap a category name to collapse or expand it. A number shows how many from that group you've selected.
⇌
Substitute ingredients
Tap the ⇌ icon on any selected chip to swap it for something else before searching.
🗑
Start over
Tap "Clear all" to deselect everything and start a fresh search.
🖨
Print shopping list
Open a recipe → scroll to Shopping List → tap "Print list".
🌐
Real recipes
Toggle "Search the web" for recipes from trusted cooking websites with source links.
```
